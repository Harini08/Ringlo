Alerts friends & family

Press the button just once, and a prepared text is sent to 3 friends and family of your choice.

Alerts emergency services

If the user presses the button for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then an emergency text with your GPS location is sent to police.

Inbuilt sensors are activated through user's pulse

If the user has pressed the button above and the sensors detect an increased heart rate of 100 beats per minute, for 3 minutes then a text with your GPS location is sent to police.



Ringlo has four sensors, including:
infrared and visible-light LEDs in addition to photosensors,
which all work together to detect heart rate.
An accelerometer inside the ring is also used for tracking movement
and since the ring is connected to your phone,
it also uses the phone's Wi-Fi and GPS data to see where you're going.
